Climate Controls, Biomes, and Climate Change

This week's focus is on understanding Norway's climate systems, the biomes that thrive there, and how climate change is affecting this northern nation. Norway's high latitude creates unique climate challenges and opportunities.

Week 5 Research Topics

• Factors controlling Norway's climate (latitude, ocean currents, topography)
• Climate zones and biomes across Norway (from coastal to alpine)
• Evidence of climate change in Norway (temperature trends, glacial retreat)
• Impacts on ecosystems, agriculture, and infrastructure
• Norway's climate policies and renewable energy initiatives
