Fluvial Processes, Oceans, and Coastlines

This week's focus is on understanding how Norway's rivers, lakes, and coastal areas are shaped by water-related processes. Norway's extensive coastline and numerous waterways make it an excellent case study for fluvial and coastal processes.

Week 4 Research Topics

• Norway's major river systems and their characteristics
• Lake formation and distribution in glaciated terrain
• Coastal processes: wave action, tidal effects, and sediment transport
• Fjord formation and oceanography
• Sea-level changes and coastal evolution since the last ice age
