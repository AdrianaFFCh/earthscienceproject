Norway

Discover Norway: Land of Fjords and Northern Lights

Norway, officially the Kingdom of Norway, is a Nordic country located in Northern Europe. Historically known for its Viking Age seafarers, Norway today combines rich cultural traditions with a modern, prosperous society. The country's economy is bolstered by natural resources, particularly petroleum and seafood, while its social model emphasizes equality and welfare. Culturally, Norway is celebrated for its contributions to art, literature, and music, deeply rooted in its stunning natural landscapes.

Explore Norway's Earth Science Wonders

From ancient geological formations to modern environmental stewardship, Norway offers a unique laboratory for understanding Earth's processes. Discover how this Nordic nation's landscape tells the story of our planet's dynamic systems.

Geological Heritage

Explore Norway's ancient bedrock, some of the oldest in Europe, shaped by billions of years of geological processes including mountain building and glaciation.

Glacial Landscapes

Witness the powerful legacy of ice sheets that carved Norway's iconic fjords, valleys, and coastal landscapes during multiple ice ages.

Living Laboratory

Study active Earth processes from coastal erosion to slope stability in a country where humans have adapted to challenging natural environments for millennia.
