Maps, Location, and Cartographers

This week's focus is on understanding Norway's position in the world, how it's represented on maps, and the history of cartography in the region. Norway's unique geographical position at the northern edge of Europe presents interesting challenges and opportunities for cartographers throughout history.

Week 1 Research Topics

• Norway's geographical coordinates and time zones
• Map projections used for Scandinavian regions
• Historical Norwegian cartographers and their contributions
• Modern mapping technologies (GIS, satellite imagery)
• How Norway's topography affects map representation
