Weathering, Mass Wasting, and Erosion

This week's focus is on understanding how Norway's landscape is shaped by weathering processes, mass wasting events, and erosion. The country's harsh climate and varied topography create unique conditions for these Earth surface processes.

Week 3 Research Topics

• Types of weathering prevalent in Norway's climate (chemical, physical, biological)
• Role of frost action and freeze-thaw cycles in rock breakdown
• Mass wasting phenomena: landslides, rockfalls, and debris flows
• Glacial and fluvial erosion processes shaping Norwegian landscapes
• Soil formation and erosion protection in Scandinavian ecosystems
